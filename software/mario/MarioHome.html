Super Mario 64 was released in 1996 as the flagship game for Nintendo's newest console, the Nintendo 64. Ever since, the game has been beloved by fans. Mario's first journey into the third dimension brought revolutionary graphics to the world of video games.


By hopping through the magical paintings hanging on the walls of Peach's Castle, Mario collects Power Stars in order to defeat Bowser and rescue the Princess. He visits many fascinating worlds, and makes some new friends on his way. Running across the plains, through the desert, diving deep under the seas and from snow and ice to lava and fire and even high above the clouds, Mario is on a quest like no other.
